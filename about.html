From C. Chochinov

ChochNotes is the #1 study website to improve your knowledge of the book Strangers by David A. Robertson (maybe because it is the only such website, but let's not talk about that). It was created by me, C. Chochinov, with the help of a certain person. That person would also like to mention that this website is open-source, and that the GitHub repository is linked in the header.

Dive deeper into the book with the themes section! I mean, who doesn't want to know more about Coley-Boley's struggles?

You can learn more about Strangers with the Literary Devices section.

You can navigate to the Interviews section to find some exclusive interviews, that, *ahem*, have been granted to us very kindly by our correspondents. We hope you will find them useful as a first-hand look into the people of Strangers.

The quizzes are also an exciting way to put your knowledge of Coley-Boley's adventures to the test (I will also add that I have scored 100% on every one of them).

Finally, there is a help video available.

That's all for me. Choch out.
