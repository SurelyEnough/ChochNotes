From C. Chochinov

ChochNotes is the #1 study website to improve your knowledge of the book Strangers by David A. Robertson, maybe because it is the only such website. It was created by C. Chochinov, with the help of a certain person. That person would also like to mention that this website is open-source, and that the GitHub repository is linked in the header.

(something about the first two elements that will have to be flushed out)

You can navigate to the Interviews section to find some exclusive interviews, that, *ahem*, may or may not have been forced out of our correspondents. We hope you will find them useful as a first-hand look into the people of Strangers.

The quizzes are also an exciting way to put your knowledge of Coley-Boley's adventures to the test. I will also add quite modestly that I have scored 100% on every one of them.

That's all for me. Choch out.
